Lasagna

Description:

Lasagna is a classic italian dish that has spread to every corner of the planet. Its simplicity and great taste with only a few ingredients makes it an obvious choice for a nice family meal. Today we will be showing you an easy recipe for 4 people to enjoy.

Ingredients (For 4 portions):

  • 8 lasagna plates
  • 500 g minced meat
  • 200 g mozzarella, in pieces
  • 200 g ricotta cheese
  • 0.5 dl grated parmesan cheese
  • 750 g tomato sauce, for pasta
  • 1 yellow onion, chopped
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp italian spices
  • salt and pepper

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 225 degrees celcius.
  2. Fry the minced meat with the onion until cooked. Mix with the pasta sauce and move to a large bowl.
  3. Whisk the egg in another bowl, mix with ricotta and italian spices.
  4. Cover an oven form (about 22 cm in diameter) with a thin even layer of oil.
  5. Break two lasagna plates and cover the bottom with these. Cover with 1/3 of the meat+sauce mix, 1/3 of the ricotta mix and 1/3 of the mozzarella. Repeat this step three more times.
  6. Sprinkle parmesan cheese over the top.
  7. Put the form in the oven about 30 minutes, until the surface is golden brown.
  8. Take out the form and serve immediately.
  9. Enjoy!

(P.S. A more time consuming but more tasty recipe uses bechamel sauce - Check it out!)
